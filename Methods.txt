Object Methods:
    * These are actions that can be performed on objects
        ** this.{variable_name} property of a person
    * Accesing the methods:
        ** objectname.methodname
        ** eg:  person.fullname();
                

String Methods:
    * These are primitive and immutable
    * str.length
    * str.charAt()
    * str.charCodeAt()
    * str.codePointAt()
    * str.concat()
    * str.at()
    * str.[.]
    * str.slice()
    * str.substring()
    * str.substr()
    * str.toUpperCase()
    * str.toLowerCase()
    * str.isWellFormed()
    * str.toWellFormed()
    * str.trim()
    * str.trimStart()
    * str.trimEnd()
    * str.padStart()
    * str.padEnd()
    * str.repeat()
    * str.replace()
    * str.replaceAll()
    * str.split()
